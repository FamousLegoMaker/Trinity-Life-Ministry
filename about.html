About us

Founded in 1985 as “Trinity House,” we operated as a homeless facility for those who wished to live in a semi-structured environment which established order and provided a stepping stone. Alcohol and drug addiction were prevalent and, in most cases, were the primary element in homelessness. In response, a Christ-centered addiction curriculum was developed and implemented. We serve over 60 men a year in our residential program and over 100 family members annually, including 50-60 children.

Trinity continues to employ a Biblical approach to life's hardships and give men the opportunity to make life changing transformations. Those in the men’s ministry are provided housing, life skills, job training, Biblical counseling, and mentoring. Each man is encouraged to find a local church for support during and after completion. Services are provided based on availability and without regard to financial status, insurance, creed, national origin, race, or religion.

Our heart's desire is to help more men overcome their life challenges and struggles through the power of Jesus Christ. Find out how you can support our mission.
